Nvidia Geforce RTX 4090

Currently the best GPU available.

The Nvidia GeForce RTX 4090 is a high-end graphics card produced by Nvidia. The 4090 offers 16,384 of the 18,432 cores and 24 GB GDDR6X graphics memory with a 384 bit memory bus and clocked at 21 Gbits. The card offers an excellent raytracing performance thanks to the 128 dedicated raytracing cores. For AI calculations (and DLSS 3 in games), the RTX 4090 has 512 Tensor cores. The gaming performance is very well suited for 4k gaming in maximum detail settings. Even Raytracing effects can be used without DLSS in most games.

Nvidia Geforce RTX 3090

Used to be the best GPU available.

The GeForce RTX 3090 is a high-end graphics card produced by Nvidia. It is part of the RTX 30 series of graphics cards and is currently one of the most powerful consumer graphics cards available. The RTX 3090 features 10496 CUDA cores, 328 Tensor Cores, and 82 RT Cores, and has a boost clock of up to 1.70 GHz. It also has 24GB of GDDR6X memory with a bandwidth of 936 GB/s, making it well-suited for demanding tasks such as 4K gaming, high-end content creation, and AI applications.

Nvidia Geforce RTX 3080

Used to be the second best GPU available.

The GeForce RTX 3080 is a high-performance graphics card produced by Nvidia. It is part of the RTX 30 series of graphics cards and is designed to offer significant improvements in gaming and content creation performance over its predecessor, the RTX 2080. The RTX 3080 features 8704 CUDA cores, 272 Tensor Cores, and 68 RT Cores, and has a boost clock of up to 1.71 GHz. It also has 10GB of GDDR6X memory with a bandwidth of 760 GB/s. It is well-suited for demanding tasks such as 4K gaming, ray tracing, and AI applications. The RTX 3080 is one of the most popular graphics cards on the market due to its high performance and reasonable price point.
